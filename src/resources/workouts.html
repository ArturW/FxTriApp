Typical Workouts

Zone 1: Recovery Mainset

(1) Recovery workout. Train steadily in Zone 1 following high-intenisty day workouts in Zone 4 and 5.

Zone 2: Aerobic Threshold Mainsets

(1) Exercise for more than 1 hour, looking for improvements over time at the same heart rate.

Zone 3: Tempo Mainsets

(1) Train steadily from 20 to 90 minutes.
(2) Tempo Intervals. Complete 30 to 90 minutes of work intervals that are 12 to 20 minutes in duration, with recovery intervals that are one-fourth long.

Zone 4: Sub-Lactate Threshold Mainsets

(1) Cruise intervals. Complete 30 to 60 mintues of intervals that are 6 to 12 minutes long, with recovery intervals that are one-fourth long.
(2) Cruise steady state. Train steadily in Zone 4 for 20-30 minutes.

Zone 5a: Lactate Threshold Mainsets

(1) Cruise intervals. Complete 30 to 60 minutes of intervals that are 6 to 12 minutes long, with recovery intervals that are one-fourth long.
(2) Cruise steady state. Train steadily in Zone 5a for 20-30 minutes.

Zone 5b: Aerobic Capacity Mainsets

(1) Aerobic capacity intervals. Complete 12-20 minutes total work intervals that are 2-4 minutes long. Recovery intervals initially are as long as the work intervals. Shorten the recovery intervals to better simulate the event.
(2) 30-30s. Alternate 30 seconds at aerobic capacity effort, pace, or power with 30 seconds at recovery effort, pace, or power. Build up to 24 such intervals over 3-4 weeks.
